Your Provider

Dr. Jonathan McConduit received his doctorate degree from Parker University, as well as earning bachelor degrees in Anatomy and Health & Wellness. With prior experience teaching Math & American Sign Language as well as earning the rank of Eagle Scout, Dr. McConduit has shown his commitment to serving the community. 

Having years of experience in Martial Arts, Dr. McConduit understands the importance of specialized care for a variety of conditions. From preventative care to individualized post trauma rehab and treatment, every patient’s care is personalized to them.

Working with all age groups and conditions from torticollis to multi-level herniated disc complexes, Dr. McConduit has developed a diversified perspective of chiropractic care and works to continually refine his techniques for better treatment.

Dr. McConduit prioritizes patient care and communication above all else and his “If it isn’t broke, don’t fix it” approach ensures his patients only get the necessary care.
